Ingredients:
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375F
2. Cream the butter and sugar together
3. Add the egg to the butter and sugar mixture
4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, cinnamon
5. Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture
6. Mix in the cocolate chips
7. Bake for 12 mins or until edges slightly brown
